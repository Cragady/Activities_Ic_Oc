How to make a sandwich, not just a sandwich, ANY sandwich!

So I will teach you how to make a sandwich as defined by today's loose definition. A few examples: knuckle sandwich, shit sandwich, meat sandwich, etc.

  1. Take any object
  2. Take a preferably different object(it could be the same, but that can be boring)
  3. Take another of the same object you picked in step one
  4. Put the different object between the two that are the same in any manner of choosing. Be it smashing or gently placing together

And there you have it. You just made a sandwich. If you want to eat your sandwich, be sure to make sure the objects of choosing are edible. If you want your sandwich to be good, make sure the edible items are complimentary to each other and is easily eaten.
